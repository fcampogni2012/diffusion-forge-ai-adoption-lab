Diffusion Forge - AI Adoption Lab

Open the interactive program: https://fcampogni2012.github.io/diffusion-forge-ai-adoption-lab/

This is an interactive visual learning lab for Everett Rogers' Diffusion of Innovations, reframed around rapid responsible enterprise AI adoption.

What it teaches

  • The core diffusion model: innovation, channels, time, and social system.
  • The five adoption attributes: relative advantage, compatibility, complexity, trialability, and observability.
  • Why opinion leaders, change agents, and champion networks matter.
  • How organizations move from adoption decision to implementation, clarification, and routinization.
  • How to use the book's ideas to accelerate AI adoption before competitors reach the new AI operating age.
